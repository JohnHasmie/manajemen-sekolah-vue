Auth · Login → Pilih Sekolah → Pilih Peran

A redesigned three-step pre-auth flow that mirrors the Kamil Edu brand identity (deep blue → cobalt → azure gradient) and respects the role color tokens (admin navy, teacher cobalt, parent azure) without imposing them on users who haven't picked a role yet. Six frames: Login (empty + error/busy), Lupa Kata Sandi sheet, Pilih Sekolah, Pilih Peran, single-school fast path. The login form drops "Ingat saya" (sessions persist via Sanctum tokens by default), surfaces Masuk dengan Google as a first-class secondary, and routes Bantuan masuk to a new help-request endpoint that emails the school admin. The KamilEdu mortarboard mark — chevrons under a flat brim with an azure bookmark tag — is rendered in inline SVG so it re-tints between the white hero card and the dark gradient bands.

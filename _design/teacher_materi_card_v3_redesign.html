Recommendation

Option A is the smallest visual change — same card silhouette, just two cleaner halves (ring + 2 tiles) instead of three. Safe pick if you want the rebuild to feel familiar.

Option B is the cleaner, more density-friendly layout — ~24dp shorter per card, no stacked stat tiles, more rows on screen. Pick this if "proper UI" means trimming the card to just the facts that matter.

Teacher · Raport · Deep redesign

Same brand chrome shipped on Buku Nilai / RPP / Jadwal / Materi — cobalt header, KPI overlap card with periode chip strip, expandable class+student cards, AppDraggableSheet for sheets, FilterChipGrid for selectors. Status pills follow the raport workflow vocabulary: Draft (amber) → Final (blue) → Terbit (green); Belum (red) when no draft exists.
